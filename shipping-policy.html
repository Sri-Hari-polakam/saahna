Logistics

Shipping Policy

Saahna SA is committed to delivering your bespoke fashion pieces with the utmost care and speed. We partner with the most reliable logistics providers to ensure your order reaches you in pristine condition.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Please note that our master tailors require this time to ensure every detail of your piece meets our rigorous quality standards. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

Secure Packaging

Every piece is wrapped in premium protective layers to maintain its silhouette during transit.

Express Delivery

We use tracked express services for all orders, providing you with real-time updates.

Shipping Rates & Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. Delivery delays can occasionally occur due to high volumes or external logistical factors, but we will always keep you informed.

International Shipping

We currently offer shipping within India. For international enquiries, please contact our concierge team directly to arrange bespoke logistics.
