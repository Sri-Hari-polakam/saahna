The Saahna Experience

Redefining luxury through personalized fashion since 2019.

Ashitha Yejju

Founder & Lead Designer

Founded in 2019 by Ashitha Yejju, SAAHNA was born from a vision to provide "complete customization from start to finish." With a deep passion for fabrics and an eye for intricate detailing, Ashitha has built SAAHNA into a premier destination for those seeking elegance and individuality.

We specialize in Fusion, Ethnic, and Western wear, ensuring that every client finds their perfect expression of style. At SAAHNA, we don't just stitch clothes; we curate experiences tailored to your personality.
