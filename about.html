The Visionary

Ashitha Yejju

Founder & Lead Designer

Founded in 2019, SAAHNA was born from Ashitha's vision to provide complete customization from start to finish. With a deep passion for fabrics and an eye for intricate detailing, she has built SAAHNA into a premier destination for those seeking elegance and individuality.

We specialise in Fusion, Ethnic, and Western wear — ensuring every client finds their perfect expression of style. At SAAHNA, we don't just stitch clothes; we curate experiences tailored to your personality.

From bridal couture to everyday luxury, every piece that leaves our studio carries the mark of master craftsmanship and a personal touch that no algorithm can replicate.

Ashitha Yejju
Our Philosophy

What Sets Us Apart

Every decision we make is guided by three core principles that define the Saahna standard.

Bespoke by Nature

No two pieces are alike. Every garment is crafted exclusively for you — your measurements, your vision, your story woven into every thread.

Uncompromising Quality

We source only the finest fabrics — from Banarasi silks to Italian linens — and pair them with master-grade tailoring techniques passed down through generations.

Personal Connection

Fashion is deeply personal. We take time to understand your lifestyle, occasions, and aesthetic before a single stitch is made — because the best fit is one that feels like you.

Conscious Craftsmanship

We believe in slow fashion — creating pieces meant to last a lifetime, not a season. Our upcycling service breathes new life into cherished fabrics.

Global Reach

From Vijayawada to the world. We serve clients across India and internationally, with virtual consultations and worldwide shipping.

Timely Delivery

We respect your time as much as your style. Every order is tracked, communicated, and delivered with precision — no surprises, only delight.
